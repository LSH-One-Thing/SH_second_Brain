
### 부정문 

#### 일반동사 부정문 
i don't work on weekends 

#### 주어가 3인칭 단수형 
- He/ She workss 
- He/ She doesn't work  
	- He doesn't cook 
	- she doesn't study 
	- Jane doesn't work 

### 복습 
- 그는 열심히 일해요 
	- He works hard 
- 그는  열심히 일을 안해요 
	- He doesn't work hard 

- 그녀는 요리를 꽤 잘해요
	- she cooks pretty well 
- 그녀는 요리를 꽤 잘해요 + 근데 매일 요리를 하지는 않아요 
	- she cooks pretty well. but she doesn't cook every day
